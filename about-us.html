About

Kolb Labs

KOLB is an activity based knowledge lab that aims to create interest and excitement in young minds towards practical education. Concepts are learnt in a simple and fun filled way through experimentation to make learning easy for students. The experiential learning methodology will encourage critical thinking and develop children as problem solvers. At a social level, by imparting these skills in a frugal way KOLBâ€™s will supplement the current education system by addressing the gap for the practical experience from a young age. We use Experiential Learning Methodology (ELM) to demonstrate experiments. All our experiments will be demonstrated using real material in the presence of students and we encourage students to try the experiments under observation from instructors. We use Videos, Prototypes and interactive discussions where it is not possible to demonstrate any experiment due to limitations.

Kolb Labs emphasizes the need of experiential learning for kids. Kolb labs offers Science Lab As A Service to schools. We visit schools and perform science experiments for class 6, class 7, class 8, class 9 and class 10 students within school campus as per CBSE, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh state syllabus. Schools need not to worry about setting up science lab and maintaining it. Instructors from Kolb Labs will bring the equipment, material required for each science experiment for class 6, class 7, class 8, class 9 and class 10 to school and demonstrate the activities as per CBSE, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh syllabus.

Experiential learning helps students to learn fast and understand the concepts clearly. More importantly, practical demonstration of science experiments will make learning fun and exciting for 6th class, 7th class, 8th class, 9th class and 10th class students. Learning by doing will help students immensely to improve their thought process. Kolb Labs encourages to adopt experiential learning at the very young age itself to influence the thought process of young minds towards innovation and creativity.

Kolb Labs will perform most of the science experiments using the real material. Kolb labs will also use other methods like images, videos, prototypes etc. Kolb labs will encourage students to perform activities hands-on. Kolb Labs also helps Class 6, Class 7, Class 8, Class 9 and Class 10 students to prepare science working models and science projects to participate in science fairs. Kolb Labs encourages students to come up with new science project ideas with knowledge gained through science activities performed.
