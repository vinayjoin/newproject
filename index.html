About

Kolb Labs

KOLB is an activity based knowledge lab that aims to create interest and excitement in young minds towards practical education. Concepts are learnt in a simple and fun filled way through experimentation to make learning easy for students. The experiential learning methodology will encourage critical thinking and develop children as problem solvers. At a social level, by imparting these skills in a frugal way KOLB’s will supplement the current education system by addressing the gap for the practical experience from a young age.

How

KOLB WAY

We use Experiential Learning Methodology (ELM) to demonstrate experiments. All our experiments will be demonstrated using real material in the presence of students and we encourage students to try the experiments under observation from instructors. We use Videos, Prototypes and interactive discussions where it is not possible to demonstrate any experiment due to limitations.

  • Hands-On Approach: Hands-On experiments using real material and equipment.
  • Learning by Observation: Play relevant videos to explain any experiment better.
  • Prototyping: Showcase prototype models where necessary.
  • Reflection: Make students to participate in concept creation and reflection after experiment.
